How to Use Payment Method Identifier (PMI)

Payment Method Identifiers(PMIs) are mainly used in web-based payment systems. When you shop online, websites list differnet ways to pay, and each option has a unique identifier.This helps the website know which paymnet method to use when you check out, making the payment process quicker and safer.

A PMI's job is to clearly identify which payment method is being used, so the transaction goes to the right place for processing.These identifier can be in differnetforms, like a simple text string or a URL, depending on the system. This flexibility allows many payment methods, such as credit cards or digital wallets, to work smoothly on a website.

For example, if you choose to pay with Apple Pay, the website will use a PMI like "https//apple.com/pay". when you select Apple pay , this identifirer is sent to the merchant's system, which knows to Use Apple pay's payment process to complet the transaction securely.
